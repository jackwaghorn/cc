Dear all,

We have received numerous complaints about our beloved county of Kent not providing enough ear-based entertainment for everyone to indulge in.

In light of such an error, Council Complaints intend to correct this by providing you with blaringly loud, horribly sweaty gigs that you won’t soon forget—even if you’d like to. We’ll also champion the best bands and artists of Kent's underground scene by releasing them here on our very own label.

You are invited to join the fun at our next party, details of which can be found on our socials or on this website.

We hope you find this response satisfactory; please don’t hesitate to let us know of any further grievance.

Kind regards,
CC.
